M.G.T.

Matlab General Toolset

View project onGitHub

Details

A set of functions developed over the years to help in MATLAB coding. Some are extremely simple and just make coding cleaner (e.g. isodd.m), others are more serious procedures (e.g. clusterLinearRegression.m).
